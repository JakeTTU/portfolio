About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Texas Tech University emphasizing on data visualization, data analytics, and numerical modeling. I enjoy creating engaging and informative visualizations and developing numerical and predictive models.

My research has been published in respected journals and conferences and had a role in projects with several U.S. Government agencies.

I am also involved in the development of decentralized applications and protocols, particularly within the Cardano blockchain ecosystem. I actively contribute to open-source projects and other porjects involving cryptographic schemes and blockchain technology.
